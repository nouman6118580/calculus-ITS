About Us



The main aim of our Calculus ITS with the intention of assisting students who struggle to understand calculus online on their own and whose parents also have a hard time instructing them to study on their own even with professional help. This is particularly crucial now when the global spread of the COVID-19 epidemic has made it impossible for primary school students to meet face-to-face for instruction. In our Calculus ITS, we solely concentrate on calculus since according to the findings of a number of previous research it is both highly challenging and very necessary to begin learning calculus at an early stage in one's educational endeavors.
